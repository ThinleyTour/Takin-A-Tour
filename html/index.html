Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking, visas, travel requirements, and more for visiting Bhutan.

Do I need a visa to travel to Bhutan?

Yes, all international visitors (except for citizens of India, Bangladesh, and the Maldives) require a visa to enter Bhutan. We handle the visa application process for you as part of your tour package.

What is the Sustainable Development Fee (SDF)?

The SDF is a daily fee collected by the Bhutanese government to support sustainable tourism. As of 2025, it is USD 100 per person per night. This fee is included in our package pricing.

When is the best time to visit Bhutan?

The best times to visit Bhutan are during spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November). These seasons offer pleasant weather, clear mountain views, and vibrant festivals.

Can I travel independently in Bhutan?

No, all international tourists must book their travel through a licensed Bhutanese tour operator. Independent travel without an approved guide or itinerary is not permitted.

What should I pack for my trip to Bhutan?

Pack layered clothing for varying altitudes and temperatures, comfortable walking shoes, a rain jacket, and modest outfits for temple visits. Sunscreen, a hat, and basic medication are also recommended.
